Routematic provides an AI-powered, end-to-end employee transportation management system for corporate companies, 

using smart routing to optimize daily commutes, reduce costs, and enhance safety through real-time tracking, automated scheduling, and a user-friendly app for employees to manage their rides, track vehicles, and get ETAs. and offers features like SOS alarms.

(Transport as a service)

For Companies (Administrators/Employers)
AI-Driven Routing: Optimizes routes using real-time traffic and employee locations to save fuel and time.
Automated Management: Manages planning, deployment, and scheduling for complex shift patterns.
Fleet & Vendor Management: Distributes trips to vendors, manages back-to-back trips, and handles billing/reconciliation.
Analytics & Reporting: Provides data on efficiency, costs, and sustainability.
Safety & Compliance: Manages driver background checks, vehicle inspections, and offers safety alerts. 

For Employees (Users)
Mobile App: Allows booking, modifying, or canceling rides.
Real-Time Tracking: See vehicle location and estimated time of arrival (ETA).
Communication: Contact drivers and support.
Safety Features: Raise SOS/panic alarms in emergencies. 
Key Benefits
Cost Efficiency: Reduces transportation costs for businesses.
Employee Satisfaction: Offers reliable, stress-free commutes, boosting morale.
Sustainability: Supports corporate goals with efficient routing and EV integration.
Scalability: Handles large numbers of employees and complex needs.
